Based on the dataset, we can see that there is a close correlation between the Total Greenhouse gases (GHG) emissions and growth of population.


A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions. Population growth, along with increasing consumption, tends to increase emissions of climate-changing greenhouse gases.
